HTX Connect
    HOME DIRECTORY REFERENCES

HTX Connect is a Houston resource directory dedicated to helping all citizens in the Houston area gain access to services benefiting their fundamental needs. As an information bank containing the locations and utilities of over 50+ organizations, HTX Connect strives to aid its users in connecting with community resources in hopes of laying the necessary groundwork for a brighter future for all. By compiling information about essential services into one accessible platform, HTX Connect empowers individuals to find support quickly and without barriers.
